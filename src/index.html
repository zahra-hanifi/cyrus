How is the staking process for Cyrus coin?

In the staking process the buyer set aside a certain amount of tokens to become an active validating node for the network and earn 2% monthly rewards which come in the form of interest.

Is it possible to subset in the stacking system?

Yes, there is the ability to invite others or subscribe to the stacking system

How the staking process could be profitable?

As Cyrus Coin is supported by the strong international sponsors such as international banks, industrial holdings , oil and gas companies and the staked investment will be traded in different financial markets in order to make more profit.

How the investors can withdraw the staking profit?

The profit will be transferred directly to the wallet of the holders on the monthly bases and it is possible to withdraw by the holder .

How long are the staking contracts?

The investing contract period is one year and the investment cannot be withdrawn till the end of the contract.

How does the subset system work?

In the subsystem, users receive 0.1 Cyrus per invitation per person . There is no referral limit and rewards credited instantly
